# Free GLB/GLTF Model Resources

Best websites to download free 3D models in `.glb` format with **no signup required** and **no payment**.

## Tier 1: Excellent (No signup, GLB available, high variety, permissive licenses)

| Site | License | Variety | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **[Poly Pizza](https://poly.pizza/)** | CC-BY/CC0 | 2,000+ models | Low-poly, instant browser download, no auth |
| **[Poly Haven](https://polyhaven.com/)** | CC0 | High-quality curated | Premium handcrafted assets, professional grade |
| **[Kenney.nl](https://kenney.nl/)** | CC0 | 40,000+ assets | Game-ready, extensive packs (characters, buildings, vehicles, etc.) |
| **[Open Source 3D Assets](https://www.opensource3dassets.com/en)** | CC0 | 991+ models | Metaverse-quality, curated, API-friendly |
| **[Quaternius](https://quaternius.com/)** | CC0 | 1,000s+ packs | Stylized, animated, game-ready (space, medieval, characters) |

## Tier 2: Good (No signup, GLB available, decent variety)

| Site | License | Variety | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **[KhronosGroup Samples](https://github.khronos.org/glTF-Assets/)** | Permissive | 100+ samples | Official glTF standard test/demo models |
| **[market.pmnd.rs](https://market.pmnd.rs/)** | CC0 | Curated | R3F ecosystem, web-optimized for Three.js |
| **[itch.io (GLB tag)](https://itch.io/game-assets/free/tag-3d/tag-glb)** | CC0/CC-BY | 100s+ assets | Community-driven, wide range of styles |
| **[GetGLB](https://www.getglb.com/)** | Various | Growing | Dedicated GLB platform, instant downloads |

## Avoid (Requires Account)

These have excellent free models but require **account creation** to download:
- **Sketchfab** — 700,000+ free models, CC licenses
- **TurboSquid Free** — 3,800+ free GLB models
- **CGTrader Free** — 150,000+ free models (342,000+ GLB total)

## Recommendation

Start with **Poly Haven** for premium quality or **Poly Pizza** for quick variety. Both require zero signup.
